Stronger Communities Through Data

Smarter urban planning

With gentrifAI, city planners are empowered with high-quality data and sophisticated modeling to help make informed, long-term proactive plans to benefit their cities. Armed with many years of forecast data and features related to gentrification, city leaders can better study the pace, geography and potential impact of changes that could affect their citizens over a long time horizon.

Development that unites

As part of the communities they serve, developers seek to improve livability through places where we live and work. The insights offered by gentrifAI empower developers to make better informed decisions that take financial, population and growth factors into play. The result is better business decisions and development that truly builds communities.

Bringing stakeholders together

A common set of data and insights make for informed stakeholders who come together for thoughtful, forward-looking discussions about the future of a city. Having high-quality data levels the playing field and brings parties together.
