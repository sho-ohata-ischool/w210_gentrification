Our Definition: What is gentrification?

Gentrification can be defined as a rapid force of cultural, economic and demographic change in cities. However, how can we define gentrification in a quantifiable way? Based on the methodology from governing.com, we are using three criteria to consider an area as gentrified.


10-Year Period

Gentrification analysis is over a period of 10 years.

Beginning of Period

Low income and low home values are below the 40th percentile.

End of Period

High home value percentage increase is above the top third percentile.
