It's Nice to Meet You!

Our team is passionate about urban development and the social implications of gentrification across the globe. Each of our team members is pursuing a master's degree in data science at UC Berkeley (MIDS), where we came together to build gentrifAI as our final capstone project. We hope this tool becomes a resource for those looking to make positive, well-informed change in city planning.
